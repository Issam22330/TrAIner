**TrAIner** is an Android fitness app that combines **workout tracking**, **exercise guidance**, **nutrition control**, and an evolving **AI assistant** in one single experience.

Built as a real mobile project, TrAIner goes beyond a typical gym log app by connecting **training data**, **nutrition data**, and **intelligent assistance** into a more useful and personalized product.

---

## ✨ Overview

TrAIner was created with one main idea in mind:

> **Bring training, nutrition, and intelligent guidance together in a single mobile app.**

Instead of forcing users to rely on different apps for tracking workouts, checking exercises, logging meals, and asking training-related questions, TrAIner unifies everything into one platform.

The app allows users to:

- log workouts in detail
- explore a full exercise catalog
- track nutrition and macronutrients
- receive support from an integrated assistant
- visualize progress over time

---

## 🚀 Main Features

### 🏋️ Workout Tracking
Track complete workouts directly from the app.

- Add exercises dynamically
- Log sets, reps, and weight
- Save training sessions in the database
- Review full workout history
- Follow your progress over time

---

### 📚 Exercise Library
A structured exercise catalog designed for both beginners and experienced users.

- **150+ exercises**
- Search bar for quick access
- Filters by muscle group
- Exercise detail screen
- Step-by-step execution guidance
- Visual support for better understanding

---

### 🍎 Nutrition Tracking
A nutrition section focused on helping users understand and control their intake.

- Personalized macro goals
- Calories, protein, carbs, and fats tracking
- Food search with external API
- Daily intake overview
- Visual progress representation
- Better awareness of nutrition habits

---

### 🤖 AI Assistant
One of the most innovative parts of the project.

TrAIner includes an integrated chatbot designed to support the user inside the app itself.

Current and planned assistant capabilities include:

- answering exercise-related questions
- explaining exercises using internal app data
- helping beginner users
- suggesting routines
- providing contextual support inside the app
- evolving into a more intelligent fitness assistant

---

## 💡 Why TrAIner is different

Most fitness apps only **store information**.

TrAIner is designed to do more:

- not just record workouts, but help users understand them
- not just show nutrition data, but make it easier to act on it
- not just include a chatbot, but integrate intelligent support into the product itself

This makes TrAIner more than a simple tracking app.

It becomes a project where **mobile development**, **data management**, **UX**, and **AI integration** come together in a practical way.
